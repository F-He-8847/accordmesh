# Code of Conduct

AccordMesh contributors are expected to keep project spaces professional, constructive, and safe.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and code, not personal characteristics.
- Give specific, evidence-based technical feedback.
- Respect privacy and never post real meeting content, credentials, or personal data.
- Acknowledge uncertainty and correct mistakes openly.
- Welcome contributors with different languages, backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints.
- Follow maintainer instructions when a discussion creates security, legal, or privacy risk.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, deliberate disclosure of sensitive data, impersonation, repeated bad-faith disruption, and knowingly unsafe instructions are not accepted.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, restrict participation, or ban accounts when necessary to protect contributors and users.
